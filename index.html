Projects

Rescue Rover

The growing urban population makes road congestion a significant barrier to timely emergency medical care, underscoring the importance of finding creative solutions. The Ambulance Clearance in Traffic (ACT) system and the Challan for Obstructive Vehicles (COV) system are two essential components of the overall solution to handle this dilemma that is presented in this article. The research also suggests an application that would improve patient access and accountability for ambulance drivers.In order to identify crowded locations and provide ambulances the best alternate routes, the ACT system makes use of real-time traffic data, GPS, and sophisticated algorithms. This reduces response times and maximizes the flow of emergency vehicles in urban environments.

Brain Tumor Detection

"Brain Tumor Detection" is a Flutter-based app that uses image processing to detect brain tumors from MRI scans. The application utilizes Google ML Kit and TensorFlow to analyze medical images and identify potential tumors with high accuracy. SQLite is employed for local data storage, managing user information and scan data efficiently. The app provides a simple, user-friendly interface for easy upload and analysis of MRI scans. It helps healthcare professionals in early tumor detection, streamlining the diagnostic process. This project showcases the integration of machine learning with medical imaging to offer an innovative solution. The goal is to make brain tumor detection more accessible and efficient. The app aims to improve healthcare outcomes.

ParkMate

"ParkMate" is a smart parking solution developed using Python, SQL, Flutter, and Google Maps, enhanced by HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The app integrates AI camera technology to detect license plates and gather owner data, streamlining parking management. It utilizes Google Maps for real-time tracking of available parking spaces, making it easier for users to find parking. SQL is used for efficient data storage and retrieval, ensuring smooth operations. The Flutter-based app offers a seamless and user-friendly experience. This project earned third place in the Startup TN Hackathon and second place in the CICS Ideathon for its innovative approach to parking. By automating processes, "ParkMate" enhances compliance and operational efficiency. It’s designed to revolutionize the parking industry.
